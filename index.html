Decision summary

This is a pilot decision. The goal is speed, clean measurement, low effort, and a clean exit if the idea does not work.

View evaluation criteria
Best first move

Run a StorifyMe proof experiment

It gives us the best balance of fast setup, a usable CMS, an acceptable dashboard fit, and manageable pilot cost (Starter plan is $99/mo.)

Keep warm

Use Storyly as the benchmark

Storyly looks more robust for advanced targeting, analytics, and scale, but it is more costly for an initial test.

Do not ignore

Run a small build-path spike

StorySDK and react-instastories can help us understand how large is the effort to build internally and what it would really cost.

Why not just choose the most powerful tool?

Storyly is stronger as a product

It is better for advanced targeting, personalization, analytics, and long-term scale.

StorifyMe is better for this pilot

The first job is to test the idea quickly, with low effort and a clean exit. That makes StorifyMe the better first move.

Important: The proof sprint must check whether StorifyMe can provide the scale, data, and control we need. If it cannot, move to Storyly.
